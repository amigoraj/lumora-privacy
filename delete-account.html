Delete your Lumora account

Two ways to request deletion — in-app or by email.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Lumora and sign in
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap Delete my account
  4. Confirm. Your account and associated data are removed within 30 days.

Option 2 — By email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address registered to your Lumora account:

Email amigoraj888@gmail.com

Include:

  • The email address on your Lumora account
  • The phrase "Please delete my account"

We reply within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, role)
  • Your learning progress, solved problems, and saved work
  • Your messages in Study Buddies and help threads
  • Your uploaded photos (if any)
  • Your device identifiers and analytics data

What we keep

  • Anonymised, aggregated statistics (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Payment records (if you had a subscription) — retained 7 years for tax law
