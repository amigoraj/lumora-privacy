# Privacy Policy

**App name:** Lumora — A Global Learning Village
**Effective date:** 15 April 2026
**Contact:** amigoraj888@gmail.com

Lumora ("we", "our", "us") is an educational app that helps students learn, teachers teach, and parents support their children. Because many of our users are children, we take privacy very seriously and comply with the U.S. **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**, the EU **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** including the children's protections under **Article 8 GDPR-K**, and the Malaysian **Personal Data Protection Act 2010**.

This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your information.

---

## 1. Who this policy applies to

Lumora has four types of accounts:

- **Students** (ages 5–18)
- **Parents / guardians**
- **Teachers**
- **School administrators**

Children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU) can only use Lumora with a verified parent or school-administrator consent. Safety features (Safe Shield, Mirror Room, Toxic Pattern Detector) are **never paywalled** — every child has access regardless of plan.

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 You give us directly

- **Account**: full name, email address, date of birth, country, role (student/parent/teacher), preferred language
- **Profile**: avatar image, school or class affiliation, study subjects, study goals
- **Learning activity**: answers you submit, photos you upload for grading, drawings and notes made inside the app, journal entries, help requests
- **Communication**: messages you send to teachers, study buddies, or classmates through the in-app channels

### 2.2 Collected automatically

- **Device information**: device model, operating system, app version, language settings
- **Technical logs**: error reports, crash diagnostics, approximate location at country level (derived from IP — we do **not** collect precise GPS)
- **Usage analytics**: features you use, time spent on activities, progress metrics (brain points, heart points) — used to power your dashboards and your teacher's analytics
- **Push-notification token** (if you enable notifications)

### 2.3 We do NOT collect

- Precise GPS location
- Phone contacts, SMS, or call logs
- Camera or microphone recordings in the background
- Health data
- Financial account details (payments go through Apple / Google / Stripe — we never see your card)
- Advertising identifiers — **Lumora has no ads and no ad tracking**

---

## 3. How we use your information

| Purpose | Examples |
|---------|----------|
| Provide the service | Show your lessons, save your answers, sync across devices |
| Educational personalisation | Recommend practice questions at your level, match you with study buddies in your subject |
| Safety | Detect toxic language in messages, age-gate features, enforce parent controls |
| Teacher & parent dashboards | Show parents your activity trends (with your consent if over 13), show teachers pass/fail statistics |
| Account security | Log you in, prevent unauthorised access, detect abuse |
| Communicate with you | Service announcements, trial expiry reminders |
| Improve Lumora | Fix bugs, prioritise new features, measure if changes work |

We do **not** use your information for behavioural advertising, profile-building for third parties, or training third-party AI models.

---

## 4. Legal bases (GDPR)

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

- **Consent** — for optional features (push notifications, sharing study answers globally in "World Classroom")
- **Contract** — to provide the service you signed up for
- **Legal obligation** — to comply with COPPA, GDPR, and Malaysian PDPA
- **Legitimate interest** — product security, fraud prevention, basic analytics (aggregated and anonymised)

For children, consent is obtained from a parent or school administrator, not the child.

---

## 5. Who we share with

We only share what is strictly necessary, and never sell personal data.

### 5.1 Service providers (processors)

| Provider | Purpose | Location |
|----------|---------|----------|
| **Supabase** | Database, authentication, file storage | EU / US |
| **Firebase Cloud Messaging** (Google) | Push notifications | Global |
| **Apple App Store / Google Play / Stripe** | Subscription payments (we never see card details) | Global |
| **Google Fonts** (self-hosted) | Typography | Global |

Each is bound by a Data Processing Agreement and may only process your data on our instructions.

### 5.2 Within the app

- **Teachers see** the work their enrolled students submit, class-level pass/fail rates, and help requests
- **Parents see** their own child's activity (subject to the child's privacy toggles if 13+)
- **Other students** only see public profile information you explicitly make visible (name, country, subjects) — never your personal data, answers, or messages

### 5.3 We will NOT share

- With advertisers
- With data brokers
- For marketing purposes
- With any third party for profit

## 6. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Lumora is designed for children and follows enhanced protections:

- **Parental consent** is required for children under 13 (US) / 16 (EU)
- **Schools** may provide consent on behalf of students under an educational-licence model
- **No behavioural advertising** or tracking across other apps/websites
- **No public profile** for children under 13 — their profile is visible only to their enrolled teacher and their own parent
- **Privacy toggles** (for ages 13+): students can hide activity details from their parent. Safety alerts are still shared.
- **Right to review and delete** — parents may request a copy of, or deletion of, all data about their child at any time
- **Data minimisation** — we only ask for the minimum needed to deliver lessons and keep children safe

If you believe a child has created an account without parental consent, email **amigoraj888@gmail.com** and we will delete the account within 30 days.

## 7. Your rights

Wherever you live, you have these rights:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Correct** — fix any inaccuracies
- **Delete** — permanently remove your account and data
- **Export** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Withdraw consent** — for anything you opted into
- **Object** — to any processing you disagree with
- **Restrict** — pause processing while a dispute is resolved
- **Complain** — to your local data-protection authority

To exercise any right, email **amigoraj888@gmail.com** with the subject line *"Privacy request"*. We respond within **30 days** (free of charge).

---

## 8. Data retention

- **Active accounts**: data is kept as long as the account is in use
- **Inactive accounts** (no sign-in for 24 months): we send a reminder; if no response, we delete the account after 30 days
- **Deleted accounts**: all personal data is removed within **30 days**; anonymised analytics may be retained indefinitely
- **Legal obligations**: some records (e.g., transaction receipts) may be kept for up to 7 years for tax compliance

---

## 9. Security

We protect your data with:

- Encryption in transit (**TLS 1.2+**) and at rest (AES-256)
- Row-level security in our database — users can only access their own data
- Supabase Auth with password hashing (**bcrypt**)
- Regular security audits and dependency scanning
- Principle of least privilege for all staff access
- No staff access to student messages or photos except to investigate a specific safety report

No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a breach affecting you, we will notify you and the relevant regulator within 72 hours (GDPR requirement).

---

## 10. International transfers

Your data may be processed in Malaysia, the European Union, or the United States. Transfers out of the EU are protected by **Standard Contractual Clauses** approved by the European Commission.

---

## 11. Payments and subscriptions

Subscriptions for Lumora Premium are processed by **Apple**, **Google**, or **Stripe**. We receive only your subscription status — never your card number, bank details, or billing address. Refunds are handled by the platform that processed your payment.

The 30-day free trial does not require a card.

---

## 12. Cookies and tracking

Lumora uses essential cookies/local storage only for:

- Keeping you signed in
- Remembering your paper type / language preferences
- Offline availability of your recent lessons

We do **not** use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics cookies that identify you personally.
